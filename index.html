TRICH (Triple Rich)

Health , Wealth & Purpose

TRICH is a performance and life architecture framework designed for high-achieving professionals who refuse to be successful in only one dimension of life.

Most people optimize for income. Some optimize for fitness. Very few intentionally build all three pillars together.

TRICH is built on the belief that true wealth is multidimensional.

Health

Without physical energy and mental clarity, achievement collapses.

TRICH Health focuses on:

Sustainable body composition and strength
Metabolic health and longevity
Mental resilience and emotional stability
Daily discipline and recovery

Health is not aesthetics. It is capacity.

Wealth

Wealth is more than money. It is leverage and relationships.

TRICH Wealth focuses on:

Income growth and capital allocation
Asset ownership and intelligent risk
Relationship capital
Reputation and professional positioning

Money gives options. Relationships multiply options.

Purpose

Purpose transforms success into significance.

TRICH Purpose focuses on:

Contribution beyond self
Structured giving and mentorship
Building institutions, not just income
Creating impact that outlives you

Purpose converts achievement into meaning.

The TRICH Standard

Each pillar is measurable.

Each pillar is trainable.

Each pillar compounds.

The TRICH Diagnostic Score (0–120) evaluates where you stand today and highlights where disciplined growth is required.

The goal is not balance. The goal is alignment.

When Health fuels Wealth,
and Wealth funds Purpose,
life moves from success to significance.

TRICH is not motivation. It is a system.
